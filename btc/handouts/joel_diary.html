September 18, 1972

Found something incredible in the basement during renovations. Workers discovered a hidden compartment behind the old fermentation tanks. Inside - a leather journal, pages yellowed but readable. German text, looks like 1920s. The previous owner, Heinrich Kessler, must have hidden it.

Had Noah translate some passages. This could be exactly what we've been looking for. References to "Der Zerteiler der Schleier" - the Render of Veils. Tommy thinks I'm crazy, but the business possibilities are endless if this is real.

Show to Nora?
October 2, 1972

Nora confirmed what Noah suspected - this is legitimate occult material. She's excited, more than I've seen her in years. The four of us agreed to investigate further. Maybe call it "research into local history" We're calling it "The Circle of the Unseen Angle."

Ordered materials from Chicago jeweler - 4 silver eye pins, custom design. $180 total. Tommy complained about the cost but I think having matching symbols will help with focus.

October 7, 1972

TEST RUN DISASTER!

We were too eager. Brought Milton Chen (travel writer - lots of "knowledge" as the ritual requires) to the basement for a preliminary attempt. Just wanted to see if anything would happen. We blindfolded ourselves as precaution, but Milton saw something when the darkness shifted.

He's completely mad now. Keeps drawing maps of places that don't exist, speaks in fragments about "angles that cut through time." We moved him to the hunting lodge - can't risk him being found like this.

Nora wants to abandon everything. Tommy's spooked. But Noah thinks we just need to be more careful with the timing.

Never again without proper preparation!
October 15, 1972

Spent week studying Heinrich's notes more carefully. The ritual requires NEW MOON - explains why October 7th was a disaster (waxing moon). Next opportunity: November 6th.

Noah calculated the exact geometric requirements. Ordered 7 obsidian pillars from specialty supplier in Minneapolis - $2,400. Tommy arranged transport through his "connections." No questions asked.

Milton is stable but still incoherent. We're keeping him fed and warm at the lodge. He'll be perfect for the real ritual.

Basement preparations complete. The seven pillars are arranged exactly per Heinrich's diagrams. Noah double-checked every angle with surveyor's equipment. The geometric pattern is... unsettling to look at directly.

Practiced the Latin pronunciations. Nora has the best accent - her university training shows. Tommy still sounds like he's ordering pizza We all need to recite in perfect unison.

8 days until new moon. Everything is ready.
